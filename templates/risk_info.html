Important Risk Warning

A "High Concern" verdict is an algorithmic indicator only. If you or someone you know is in crisis or at immediate risk, please seek professional help right away.

1. What the Verdict Means

The "High Concern" verdict indicates that the analyzed content shows linguistic patterns that are statistically similar to those found in training data associated with distress or risk-related language. This includes:

  • Higher-than-usual negative or self-referential wording.
  • Repeated expressions of hopelessness or withdrawal.
  • Patterns detected by the abnormality model (Na√Øve Bayes classifier).

This is a probability-based signal produced by machine learning and sentiment analysis. It is not a psychological diagnosis, clinical judgement, or confirmation of any condition.

2. Limitations of This Tool

MindSpace has several important limitations:

  • It only processes the public posts that were successfully scraped.
  • It cannot see private messages, deleted posts, or offline behaviour.
  • Models may be affected by sarcasm, jokes, cultural context, or incomplete data.
  • Results can contain false positives or false negatives.

Because of these limitations, the output should be viewed as a technical signal, never as final evidence or a label placed on any person.

3. Professional Help & Next Steps

MindSpace is not a substitute for clinical, medical, or psychological advice. If the content you analyzed worries you, consider the following:

  • Encourage the person (or yourself) to talk to a licensed mental health professional.
  • Reach out to a trusted family member, friend, or support network.
  • Use local helplines or crisis services when immediate support is needed.

Only trained professionals can properly evaluate risk, provide a diagnosis, or recommend treatment.

4. In an Emergency

If you believe there is an immediate risk of harm to yourself or someone else:

  • Contact your local emergency number right away.
  • Reach out to a crisis hotline available in your region.
  • Seek help from nearby emergency services, hospital, or clinic.

Do not rely on this website or its reports in any urgent or life-threatening situation.
