Privacy Policy

MindSpace is designed as a technical analysis tool and respects your privacy. All processing is performed only on public data that you explicitly choose to analyze.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the MindSpace web application, including:

  • Accounts you create (username and password).
  • Analysis history generated while you are logged in.
  • Public social media content fetched based on usernames or profile URLs you provide.

2. Data Sources

MindSpace only analyzes publicly available content:

  • Public Reddit comments from the username you enter.
  • Public Instagram posts/captions from a username or profile URL.
  • Public tweets from a Twitter/X handle.

No private messages, private profiles, or hidden data are accessed by this system.

3. Data Storage

When you are logged in, the following information may be stored in the local SQLite database:

  • Your account details: chosen username and a securely hashed password.
  • Analysis records: platform, target username/profile, verdict, and aggregated scores.
  • Full JSON results for each analysis, including post texts and computed metrics.

This data is stored locally for history and PDF report generation. We do not sell, rent, or share these records with third parties.

4. Live Analysis & Session Data

During a live analysis, data is temporarily kept in the current session:

  • Scraped posts and computed scores are held in memory to render graphs and summaries.
  • The latest analysis may be placed in the session to support instant PDF download.

After the session ends or you leave the site, this temporary session data is discarded. Only records you explicitly run while logged in are stored in your history.

5. Data Deletion & Account Removal

You may request deletion of:

  • Your user account (username and login credentials).
  • All associated analysis history and stored JSON results.

To request deletion, please contact the project maintainer at support@mindspace.demo .

6. Use Disclaimer

MindSpace is a technical demonstration tool. The analyses and reports it generates are based on statistical and linguistic models and are not medical, clinical, or psychological diagnoses. Do not use the results as a substitute for professional help.
